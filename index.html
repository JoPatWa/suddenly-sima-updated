Suddenly Sima is a travel blog, I have a travel bug I cannot kick love to blog about my experiences. It is also a beauty blog. Firstly, I am an african woman who is proud of her heritage. I believe beauty comes from the inside but is augmented by makeup, clothing, and style. Lastly, Suddenly Sima is a Lifestyle blog. Expect posts concerning my interests, opinions, and behaviours. While those three categories are my bread and butter, there will be instances where I want to blog about something that does not fit into those categories. For example, DIY or do-it-yourself blog posts. I am actually pretty crafty! All of this and more served with a fun twist.
