This is a home for 200 million people and also known to be a home to several self-made billionaires. Perth is well known for its botanic gardens, arts galleries and beautiful white sand beaches accompanied by nice warm weather. It arts scene is catching up on Sydney’s. In this city there are many things you will have fun exploring. This side of Australia is comprised of historical and modern setting: The 19th century masons and breathtaking skyscrapers. Perth holds a sense of community, in this city most people are friendly: you will notice them exchanges hello’s when passing. While strolling around you may want to check out Kings Park & Botanic Garden which displays over 3000 unique plants species.


During your stay in Perth don’t forget to book yourself a cruise in the Swan River, whilst on board you will enjoy a breath taking view of the city, million dollar homes and city skylines. The place offers plenty outdoors activities, from biking, boat paddling, snorkeling and etc. You will sure have a great time in Perth, especially if you like the beaches and clean white sands then you are in luck because there is plenty of that, starting with the iconic beaches: On the west coast is Scarborough Beach and about 12 minutes’ drive down south along West Coast Drive is Cottesloe Beach.


In addition to that, did you know that Western Australia has its own island? That’s right, Rottenest Island is its name, and beautiful is not even a name to describe beauty. Rottnest is car free so if you think you can’t handle relying on your ten toes for transportation I would encourage you to consider renting a bike from a ferry company. It a great destination for water sports and has a vast marine life to check out. Did I mention that the island is a home to a happiest cute animal known as a quokka? They are so cute and easy to get along. Omg! Just by writing about Perth is exciting I even forgot to mention foods!
