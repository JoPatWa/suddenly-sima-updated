ne of the things I’m passionately while traveling are local joints, a hole in the wall type of place because I know that where traditional and a local food is served. Nothing beat the experience of entertaining your taste buds with ethnic food. A best trick that has helped me through the years while traveling is to minimize eating hotel food because, in my opinion, food that is served at the hotel is modified by top their chefs to the point that it taste just okay not as authentic as the food you will find at a local joint down the street. While traveling I like to check out local hangout that where you get ideas and recommendations of the best place to grab something off from a “mom’s and papa’s” type of restaurant. I really like local orientated type restaurants because other than having reasonable prices they offer variety of ethnic food.
