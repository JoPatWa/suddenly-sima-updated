Why I Created This Blog

 I decided to create this blog to share the knowledge I gained through my traveling experience in hope of inspiring you to get out there and meet the world but most of all I created this blog to offer you tools that will help you get through this unforgiving life.

 Fashion is one of my hobbies. I enjoy putting different pieces together to see what comes out. My understanding of fashion isn't defined by fashion trends but my own unique taste.

 As far as beauty goes it's safe to say I love beauty and skin care. I'm fascinated by the science behind beauty. I want to learn about beauty trends, techniques, products, and innovations. I’m just a black woman who's trying to protect her normal-to-dry, eczema prone skin from the heat of the sun and winter winds. This means I do a lot of research, asking of questions and I try out a lot of products. I even create some of my own. I’m neither a doctor nor an esthetician – the closest medical degree I have would probably be awarded for watching medical shows on TV.
